The song that took Kanye to fame

Through The Wire is a song on his debut album, The College Dropout, where he talks about a nearly fatal car crash he was involved in. The medical workers taking care of Kanye at the time had to put a wire through his jaw, and he made the track about the whole incident. The inspiring story and thoughtful lyrics were really the things that pushed kanye west into the spotlight.


Controversies

Kanye West, in around October 2022, tweeted some pretty bad stuff about jews and anti-semitism. This was followed up by a... carnival of interviews, where he'd just get mad or walk out for the attention of the media. Around a year later, on December 26, 2023, he tweeted out an apology to the Jewish community, about how he is looking to improve himself.


Whether it's controversy, good music, or meaningful lyrics, this man has it all.
