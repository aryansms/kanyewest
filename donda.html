Donda

Kanye West's "Donda", released in 2021, was more than just an album; it was a tribute to his late mom and a reflection of his own journey through grief and healing. With tracks like "Hurricane" and "Jail", Kanye explored themes of redemption, personal transformation, drawing listeners into his world with raw honesty and vulnerability, similar to Ye.
Collabs with artists like Jay-Z and Kid Cudi added layers of depth and emotion to the album. This finishes our journey throughout Kanye's discography, at an astonishing count of 10 solo albums right now. All of them soldify Kanye West's status as one of the most influential artists of his generation.
