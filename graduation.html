Graduation

Kanye West's Graduation, released in 2007, wasn't just a collection of songs; it was a celebration of success and perseverance. With tracks like "Stronger" and "Good Life," Kanye elevated the genre of hip-hop. It wasn't just about the music; it was about Kanye's evolution as an artist. This is the album that got me into listening to Kanye West, and I must say it's kind of nice to start here because of all the easy listening tracks on the album.
