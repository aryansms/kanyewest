Jesus Is King

In 2019, Kanye West surprised the world with "Jesus Is King," a gospel-inspired album that marked a radical departure from his previous work. This wasn't just a rap album; it contained lyrics that spoke of faith, redemption, and the power of God's love. Tracks like "Follow God" and "Closed on Sunday" showcased Kanye's newfound devotion to Christianity, inviting listeners to join him on a journey of self-discovery and enlightenment.
