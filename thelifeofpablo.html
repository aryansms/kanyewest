The Life of Pablo

Released in 2016, "The Life of Pablo" set out on a musical journey unlike anything he had ever done before. This wasn't just an album; it was a work of art, constantly evolving and getting better with each track.
From the gospel-infused songs like Ultralight Beam to the depth of Real Friends, Kanye invited listeners to his inner thoughts and emotions.
