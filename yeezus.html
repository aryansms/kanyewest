Yeezus

In 2013, Kanye West unleashed "Yeezus" upon the world, shattering expectations and defying categorization. This wasn't just another rap album; it was an experience, with abrasive production and lyrics that challenged listeners to rethink everything they knew about hip-hop music. Songs like "Black Skinhead" and "Bound 2" pushed the boundaries of the rap genre, immortalizing Kanye's status as a true innovator in the world of hip-hop. People were expecting something like MBDTF, with beautiful samples, but Kanye decided to take a different approach with this album.
